My Name is Mud

The First Book in the Mud Mystery Series

Rejected for not being Indian enough, Kiowa born Mud escapes to Silicon Valley where she transforms into successful professional Mae. It takes murder, theft and the disappearance of her Grandfather to get Mud back to the Reservation of her childhood. Upon arrival, she’s thrown into an unexpected spiritual quest as she faces childhood foes and her own insecurities, deceit and a charging buffalo. It takes a blend of Silicon Valley ingenuity and Native American spirituality to solve the murder—before time runs out.

The Mud Mystery Series

Murder with a Twist of Kiowa Spirituality

My Name is Mud, is the first in a planned murder mystery series. The first four books follow successive days with each flowing to the next emulating stages in a Kiowa spiritual vision quest as Mud stumbles, rights herself and finds her place in both worlds, Silicon Valley and Kiowa Country, all while solving murders. The quest begins in My Name is Mud with Mud discovering her Spirit Animal while she struggles to solve a murder in Kiowa Country.

Each of the first four books in the Mud Mystery Series can be read as stand-alone novels. But when enjoyed as a series, there are threads connecting each story and extra clues starting in one that extends across to the following novels. At the end of the fourth novel, Mud finds a way to unite her two worlds while solving another murder mystery.

Mud's adventures won't end at the fourth book. More Mud murder mysteries with a twist of Kiowa are planned.
